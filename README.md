# 🏥 Youth Smoking and Drug Dashboard 🚭💊

### 📊 Interactive Excel Dashboard for Analyzing Smoking & Drug Use Trends

![Dashboard Screenshot](./Screenshot.png)

## 🔍 Overview  
This dashboard provides an **interactive visualization** of smoking and drug usage trends among different **age groups, genders, and counseling accessibility levels**.  

It allows users to track:  
- 📈 **Trend Analysis** – Smoking & drug experimentation rates across years.  
- 🚻 **Gender Disparities** – Comparing smoking/drug use between males and females.  
- 🏫 **Impact of School Programs & Counseling** – Does accessibility to counseling reduce substance use?  
- 👪 **Parental Support Effect** – Understanding the role of family background.  

---

## 🚨 Problem Statements  

1. **How has smoking and drug experimentation changed over the years?**  
   → Analyzing trends in smoking and drug use from 2020 to 2024.  

2. **Are males or females more likely to smoke or experiment with drugs?**  
   → Comparing gender-based differences in substance use.  

3. **Does school counseling reduce smoking and drug use?**  
   → Evaluating whether students with access to counseling programs show lower rates of smoking and drug use.  

4. **How does parental support impact smoking and drug habits?**  
   → Analyzing if individuals from supportive family backgrounds have lower addiction rates.  

5. **Which age group is at the highest risk?**  
   → Identifying the most vulnerable age groups for early intervention.  

---

## 📊 Key Insights  

🔹 **Rising Drug Experimentation**: A noticeable increase in drug use among youth after 2022.  
🔹 **Gender Differences**: Males have a higher smoking rate, but drug experimentation is nearly equal across genders.  
🔹 **Counseling Programs Help**: Students with access to counseling show a **15-20% lower** smoking prevalence.  
🔹 **Parental Influence**: Youth with **low parental support** have significantly higher smoking and drug use rates.  
🔹 **Most At-Risk Age Group**: **15-24 years** shows the highest increase in substance use, requiring urgent intervention.  

---

## 🎯 Features  

✅ **Slicers for Year & Gender** – Filter data dynamically.  
✅ **Line Charts** – Track trends in smoking & drug usage over time.  
✅ **Bar & Pie Charts** – Compare age group statistics and gender distribution.  
✅ **Funnel Chart** – Analyzing smoking and drug experimentation stages.  

---

## 📂 Dataset  
The dataset includes:  
- **Age Groups** (10-14, 15-19, etc.)  
- **Smoking Prevalence (%)**  
- **Drug Experimentation (%)**  
- **Gender-based Data**  
- **School Counseling Accessibility**  

---

## 📊 How to Use  
1. Open the **Excel file** containing the dashboard.  
2. Use the **Year Slicer** to filter data for specific years.  
3. Select **Gender** to compare male vs. female insights.  
4. Analyze trends and insights from different visualizations.
